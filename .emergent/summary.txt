**what currently exists?**
A full-stack web application has been developed using FastAPI for the backend and React for the frontend. The application is branded for Victoria Eventos, incorporating its logo and a color scheme (gold, beige) and typography (Playfair Display) inspired by their website.

The application includes:
-   **Authentication:** JWT-based login/registration for Planner and Couple roles.
-   **Dashboards:** Separate dashboards for planners (viewing all events) and couples (viewing their single event).
-   **Event Management:** Planners can create, view, and delete events.
-   **Core Modules (within an event):**
    -   **Resumen (Summary):** Displays client info, contract date, and contracted services.
    -   **Tareas (Tasks):** Features an automatically generated checklist of 43 tasks for weddings, grouped by timeline (e.g., 12-9 months before). Deadlines are calculated based on the contract date.
    -   **Invitados (Guests):** Manage guest list and RSVPs.
    -   **Presupuesto (Budget):** Track budget items and record multiple payments against them.
    -   **Proveedores (Suppliers):** A predefined list of 19 supplier types that can be assigned to an event.
    -   **Notas (Notes):** A communication feed between planner and couple.
    -   **Galería (Gallery):** Generate and display inspirational images using OpenAI (gpt-image-1).
    -   **Archivos (Files):** Upload and download PDF, JPG, and PNG files.
